The objective in Container Adventure is to match three or more identical products on a shelf and eliminate them. Each level presents different goals—clearing specific items, reaching a score threshold, or removing obstacles. As you progress, the puzzles become more complex, requiring thoughtful moves and occasional use of boosters. There’s also a sense of progression through unlocking new supermarket areas. The end goal? Becoming a master of shelf organization with the highest scores and best strategies.

Boosters are special tools designed to help you overcome difficult levels. Some clear rows or columns, others shuffle the board or target specific items. You can earn them by completing levels or purchase them using in-game currency. Timing is important—save boosters for when you're low on moves or stuck with limited matching options. Used correctly, a single booster can completely change the course of a level. Remember, strategic use is better than random tapping.
